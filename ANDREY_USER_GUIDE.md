## 💬 Dialog Screen — Your Main Lesson

This is where the learning happens! You practice through **live conversations**.

### Your Three Characters

| Avatar | Name | Role |
|--------|------|------|
| 🔴 **L** | **Léon** | Native Luxembourgish speaker — your teacher |
| 🟠 **A** | **Andrey** | You! Starting from zero |
| 🟣 **J** | **John** | Level A2 — your target in 6 months |

### Two Dialog Tabs

- **👤 Andrey** — Your dialog with Léon, step by step
- **👨 John A2** — The same dialog at A2 level — this is your goal!

### Buttons in the Dialog

| Button | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **▶** (green circle) | Léon speaks the phrase |
| **0.7×** | Slow speed — great for learning new phrases |
| **1×** | Normal speed (active by default) |
| **🎤 Говорю!** (after Léon) | Record yourself repeating Léon's phrase |
| **🎤 Говорю!** (after Andrey/John) | Record yourself saying your own line |
| **▶ Léon** | Listen to Léon's pronunciation again |
| **▶ Я** | Play back YOUR recording |

### How to Practice — Step by Step

```
1. Press ▶ — listen to Léon carefully
2. Read the phrase aloud using the phonetics [in brackets]
3. Press 0.7× — listen again at slow speed
4. Press 🎤 Говорю! — record yourself saying the phrase
5. Press ▶ Léon — hear the original again
6. Press ▶ Я — hear your own voice
7. Compare! Repeat until it sounds similar
8. Move to the next line
```

### Phonetic Guide

Under each Luxembourgish phrase you'll see Russian phonetics in **[ ]** brackets:

```
Moien, Andrey!
[Мо́йен, Андрэй!]
Good morning, Andrey!
```

The **accent mark ́** shows which syllable to stress — hold it slightly longer.

## 🎤 Voice Recording — How It Works

The recording feature lets you compare your pronunciation with Léon.

### First Time Use
Your browser will ask permission to use the microphone.  
Tap **"Allow"** — without this, recording won't work.

### Recording Steps
1. Tap **🎤 Говорю!**
2. Button changes to **⏹ Стоп** — speak your phrase now
3. Tap **Стоп** when finished (or it stops automatically after 8 seconds)
4. A player panel appears below the button

### Compare Your Pronunciation
After recording:
- **▶ Léon** — hear the native speaker
- **▶ Я** — hear yourself
- Listen to both and notice the differences
- Record again until you're happy!

### If Microphone Doesn't Work

**Android Chrome:**
- Settings → Apps → Chrome → Permissions → Microphone → Allow

**iPhone Safari:**
- Settings → Safari → Microphone → Allow

## 📝 Exam — Sproochentest Practice

The **Sproochentest** is the real Luxembourg language exam required for citizenship.  
This trainer includes a **model exam** you can take at any time!

### How to Access the Exam
- Tap **📝 Экзамен** in the bottom navigation bar
- Or tap the **"Model Exam"** button on the Home screen

### What the Exam Includes
- **10 questions** covering listening comprehension, vocabulary, and grammar
- Questions match the real Sproochentest format
- Each question has a hint to guide you

### Question Types
| Type | What you do |
|------|------------|
| 🎧 Listening | Press ▶ to hear a phrase, then choose the correct response |
| 📖 Vocabulary | Choose the correct translation or meaning |
| 📝 Grammar | Fill in the blank or complete the sentence |

### After the Exam
- See your score: how many correct out of 10
- **Passing score: 70%** (7 out of 10)
- Every answer shows detailed explanation — right or wrong
- Tap **"Try again!"** for another attempt — unlimited retries!

### Exam Statistics
Your results are saved automatically:
- ✅ Passed exams counter
- ❌ Failed exams counter  
- % Success rate

You can see your stats on the **Progress screen** and on the Home screen.

## 🔊 Voice Setup — German Accent

Luxembourgish (`lb-LU`) is not available in standard browser speech engines, so the app uses **German voice (de-DE)** — the closest available sound.

When you first open the app, you'll see a voice check dialog.

### Install German Voice on Android
1. Settings → General Management → Language & Input
2. Text-to-Speech → Google TTS ⚙️
3. Language → Add **Deutsch (Deutschland)**
4. Restart Chrome

### Install German Voice on iPhone
1. Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices
2. Find **German → Anna**
3. Download → restart Safari

### Install German Voice on Windows
1. Settings → Time & Language → Language
2. Add language → **Deutsch**
3. Check "Text-to-speech" → Install
4. Restart Chrome

## 💡 Top Tips for Fast Progress

### Daily Routine (15 minutes)
```
✅ 5 min  — Review words from yesterday's lesson
✅ 7 min  — Work through today's dialog (record yourself!)
✅ 3 min  — Complete the exercises
```

### How to Learn Fastest
- **Say everything out loud** — don't just listen and read
- **Use 0.7×** for new phrases, switch to 1× once you know them
- **Record yourself daily** — you'll hear your own improvement
- **Do the exam regularly** — even from lesson 1!
- **Check John's dialog** — that's your A2 goal in 6 months

### Luxembourgish Learning Secret
Luxembourgish is very similar to German. If you already know some German (even basic), you have a huge advantage — many words are nearly identical!

## ❓ Quick FAQ

**Q: Does it work without internet?**  
A: The app needs internet to load the first time. After that, it works mostly offline.

**Q: Is my progress saved?**  
A: Yes — automatically saved on your device. Don't clear browser data.

**Q: Can I use it on multiple devices?**  
A: Yes, but progress is saved separately on each device.

**Q: Why does the voice sound slightly German?**  
A: Luxembourgish isn't available in browsers yet. German is the closest — and actually helps because lb and de are sister languages!

**Q: How many times can I take the exam?**  
A: Unlimited! The exam is available from day one and you can retry as many times as you want.

**Q: When will lessons 26-77 be ready?**  
A: Coming soon! The A2 and B1 blocks are being written now.
